Privacy Policy

Your money log stays intentionally small.

Monee is designed as a lightweight, voice-first expense tracker. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, when data stays on your device, when limited data is sent to external services to power AI parsing, and what optional syncing features are available.

1. Information we handle

Monee handles information that is necessary to create, organize, and sync your expense records. Depending on the features you use, this may include:

  • Expense and income records you create manually or confirm after AI parsing, including title, amount, category, date, notes, and source text.
  • Receipt photos or other attachments you choose to add to a record. When iCloud Sync is enabled, supported attachment files may also sync through your private Apple iCloud account.
  • Text you speak or type when using voice input or manual input.
  • Text recognized from receipt images. Receipt OCR itself is performed on-device using Apple frameworks before any text is sent for AI parsing.
  • Custom categories, app preferences, notification preferences, profile display name and bio, widget snapshot data, and purchase entitlement state.
  • Limited purchase and transaction status information provided by Apple through StoreKit so the app can unlock Monee Premium features.
Important detail about receipts

Monee currently performs optical character recognition on-device. When you use AI receipt parsing, the extracted text may be sent to the parsing service, but the raw receipt image itself is not uploaded by the current implementation for parsing.

2. How we use information

We use information handled by Monee to operate the app and deliver the features you request.

  • To create, store, edit, display, and delete your records on your device.
  • To generate summaries, widgets, charts, and insights from your saved records.
  • To request microphone, speech recognition, photo library, camera, notification, and iCloud access only when you use features that require them.
  • To process natural-language text and OCR-extracted receipt text through the AI parsing pipeline so the app can suggest structured entries.
  • To sync supported records, attachment files, custom categories, and selected profile and preference data through iCloud when you enable iCloud Sync.
  • To confirm and restore your paid Monee Premium access through Apple.

3. External services and sharing

Monee does not sell your personal data. The app does, however, rely on a small number of third-party services when specific features are used.

  • Apple services. Apple may process microphone, speech recognition, iCloud, notification, and purchase-related data according to Apple's own terms and privacy policies.
  • Cloudflare Workers. AI parsing requests are routed through a Monee-operated Cloudflare Worker endpoint.
  • DeepSeek. The current AI parsing backend forwards parsing requests to DeepSeek to transform your text into structured expense data.

In the current implementation, the information shared externally for AI parsing is the text you provide, plus related context such as locale, currency, time, and custom category names. Payment card details are handled by Apple, not by Monee.

No advertising SDKs in the current version

Based on the current app implementation, Monee does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not include behavioral analytics tracking inside the app.

4. Storage, sync, and retention

Monee is local-first. Most app data is stored on your device. Some features add optional syncing or external processing:

  • Local storage. Records, settings, custom categories, widgets, avatars, and attachments are stored on-device so the app can work locally.
  • Optional iCloud sync. If you enable iCloud Sync, supported expense record data, attachment files, custom categories, profile details, avatar image, currency selection, and budget settings may sync through your private Apple iCloud account using CloudKit.
  • Widget data. Widget snapshot data is stored in a local shared app group so the widget extension can render current summaries.
  • Notifications. If you enable reminders, the app stores your notification preference and schedules local notifications on your device.

You can delete records from inside the app, disable notifications, disable iCloud Sync, or uninstall the app. Copies that are stored or processed by Apple or other service providers may be retained under their own policies for a limited time.

5. Your choices

  • You can use core parts of Monee without creating an account.
  • You can deny microphone, speech recognition, photo library, camera, notifications, or iCloud permissions, but related features may not work.
  • You can disable iCloud Sync in Settings. In the current implementation, changes to the iCloud setting apply on next app launch.
  • You can review AI-generated results before saving them.
  • You can delete records and clear stored data from within the app.

6. Children

Monee is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please use the support contact listed on the App Store page or your product support channel.
